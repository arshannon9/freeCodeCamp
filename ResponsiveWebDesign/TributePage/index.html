Prof. Dr. George M. A. Hanfmann

Classical Archaeologist

Here's a time line of Dr. Hanfmann's life:

  • 1911 - (November) Born in St. Petersburg, Russia
  • 1921 - Emigrates to Germany
  • 1934 - Receives D.Phil. from Friedrich-Wilhelms-Universität, Berlin, marries his wife Ilse, and emigrates to the United States
  • 1935 - Receives Ph.D. from Johns Hopkins University under David Moore Robinson, and elected as Junior Fellow to the Harvard Society of Fellows
  • 1936 - Publishes his D.Phil. dissertation as Altetruskische Plastik I. Die menschliche Gestalt in der Rundplastik bis zum Ausgang der orientalisierenden Kunst (Konrad Triltsch, Würzburg)
  • 1940 - Naturalized as a U.S. citizen
  • 1943 - Begins service as U.S. radio editor for the Office of War Information in London, and as chief of the German section for broadcasting to the Continent under the American Broadcasting Staion in Europe (ABSIE)
  • 1986 - (13 March) Dies at the age of 74.
